This archive investigates how contemporary artists engage with the technologies, infrastructures, and imaginaries of outer space. It collects artworks, critical essays, and documentation that explore satellites as aesthetic, political, and planetary media. The project aims to trace how orbital perspectives transform artistic practice and reshape our understanding of earthbound existence.# Satellite-Art-Archive
A research-based archive exploring the intersection of art, technology, and outer space, documenting artistic practices that engage with satellites and orbital imaginaries.
