The Satellite Art Archive is a research-driven platform exploring the intersections of art, technology, and outer space. It aims to collect, document, and interpret creative practices that engage with satellite systems, orbital imaginaries, and the aesthetics of planetary observation.

This archive is part of a larger inquiry into how human and nonhuman actors — from artists to machines in orbit — participate in the shaping of contemporary visual culture and cosmological thought.

Curated and maintained by gemige530-star, the project welcomes collaborations and contributions from researchers, artists, and engineers.
