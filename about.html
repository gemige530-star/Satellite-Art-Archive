About Satellite Art Archive

Tracing the artistic imagination of orbit and beyond.

Satellite Art Archive is a research-based initiative devoted to exploring how art expands into the orbital realm. It documents, interprets, and curates projects where artists collaborate with engineers, space agencies, and cosmic environments to redefine what art can be.

This platform connects the technical with the poetic, following the trajectories of artworks that travel beyond Earth — from early conceptual experiments to present-day satellite performances and lunar transmissions.

Curated by gemige530-star

Founder and researcher of the Satellite Art Archive, gemige530-star investigates how orbital technologies influence aesthetic thought, data circulation, and planetary consciousness.

Their work bridges digital archiving, media art, and post-terrestrial theory — creating a space where artistic practice meets cosmic infrastructure.
