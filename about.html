About Satellite Art Archive

Tracing the artistic imagination of orbit and beyond.

Satellite Art Archive is a research-driven platform dedicated to documenting and interpreting how contemporary artists engage with the technologies, infrastructures, and imaginaries of outer space. It traces the aesthetic, political, and ecological dimensions of orbital art practices.

The project examines artistic works that involve satellites, space stations, and lunar experiments — including performances, data transmissions, and installations that blur the boundaries between Earth and orbit, human and machine, art and science.

Curated and maintained by gemige530-star, the archive aims to create a growing constellation of references and artworks that reveal how orbital perspectives transform artistic imagination and our understanding of life on Earth.
