Satellite art refers to artistic practices that occur in satellite orbits and on the lunar surface. These include satellites launched for artistic purposes, art performances conducted aboard the International Space Station, and artworks designed to be sent to the Moon.

This website serves as a research-based archive for collecting and organizing data related to satellite art projects worldwide. The archive is currently in its trial phase, and its structure and content are being gradually expanded.
